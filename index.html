Committed to Environmental Responsibility

DC Environmental Consultancy Services is a private, independent professional consulting firm delivering specialized technical documentation, environmental impact assessments, and regulatory compliance assistance across the Philippines.

All our applications are submitted strictly through official government channels. We maintain the highest standards of integrity, professionalism, and environmental stewardship in every engagement.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted environmental consultancy in the Philippines, recognized for excellence, integrity, and environmental stewardship.

Our Process

How It Works

From your first inquiry to approved permits — here's what working with us looks like.

01

Inquiry & Scoping

Tell us about your project. We assess the scope, determine which permits apply, and send you a clear proposal — no hidden fees.

02

Field Work & Assessment

Our team conducts on-site surveys, sampling, and data gathering — air, water, soil, biodiversity, and social baseline studies.

03

Documentation

We prepare all required technical reports, EIS/IEE documents, engineering plans, and compliance forms for submission.

04

Permit Approval

We handle filing with DENR, LLDA, NWRB and other agencies, follow up on status, and guide you until your permit is released.
