Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA / EIS)

Comprehensive evaluation of the environmental effects of proposed projects, including land, water, air, and social impact modules.

Environmental Monitoring Programs

Design and implementation of systematic monitoring programs to track environmental parameters and ensure ongoing compliance.

EMPIMPPEMAPS

Air, Water & Noise Assessment

Scientific measurement and analysis of air quality, water quality, and noise levels to identify and address environmental concerns.

Air QualityWater QualityNoise

Baseline & Biodiversity Studies

BaselineBiodiversityGIS Mapping

IEC, FGD, KII & IPS Activities

Facilitation of information, education, and community engagement activities including focus group discussions and key informant interviews.

IECFGDKIIIPS

Traffic & Geohazard Assessment

Traffic Impact Assessment (TIA) and Engineering Geological and Geohazard Assessment Reports for project planning and safety.

TIAEGGAR

Environmental Compliance Certificate (ECC)

Processing of ECC through Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) and Initial Environmental Examination (IEE), including minor and major amendments.

ECCEISIEE

Certificate of Non-Coverage (CNC)

Assistance in obtaining CNC documentation for projects that do not require a full environmental impact assessment under DENR regulations.

CNCDENR

Pollution Control & Air Permits

PCO Accreditation, Permit to Operate for Air Pollution Source Installation (PTO-APSI), and Wastewater Discharge Permit (WWDP) processing.

PCOPTO-APSIWWDP

Hazardous Waste Permits

Hazardous Waste Generator ID (HWG-ID), Permit to Transport Hazardous Waste, and Hazardous and Solid Waste Management Plan preparation.

HWG-IDWaste Transport

LLDA Permits

LLDA Clearance, LLDA Discharge Permit, Shoreland Development Clearance (SDC), and Fish Pen Privilege to Operate — processed through the Laguna Lake Development Authority.

LLDASDCDischarge Permit

NWRB Water Permits

National Water Resources Board (NWRB) Water Permit and Surface Water Permit processing for groundwater extraction, irrigation, and other water resource uses.

NWRBWater PermitSurface Water

Site Development Plan (SDP)

Preparation of Site Development Plans with sanitary and architectural sign and seal by licensed professional engineers.

SDPAs-built

Floor Plan & Drainage Layout

Detailed floor plan layouts, drainage systems, and septic tank designs for commercial, residential, and industrial projects.

Floor PlanDrainageSeptic

Licensed Engineer's Reports

Engineer's Reports signed and sealed by licensed professionals, covering structural, sanitary, and environmental specifications.

Sign & SealLicensed PE

Construction Site Environmental Compliance

On-site environmental compliance monitoring for construction projects, ensuring adherence to DENR and local government standards.

ConstructionCompliance

Industrial Environmental Management

Comprehensive environmental management for industrial facilities including waste management plans, pollution monitoring, and regulatory reporting.

IndustrialWaste MgmtMonitoring
